News Classifier and Authenticator

Internet has made access to news and information all around the world relatively easier. It has forced us to redefined our definition of privacy. More and more people are relying on internet and social media for information and support but few of them realise the negative side of it. A lot of misleading news is distributed on social media and is regularly propagated in the regular media .

The goal of this project is to create a fidelity detector that is based on the AI algorithm- multinomial NB classifier. The aim was to find out the suitability of this method to solve this particular problem if provided with a manually labelled news dataset and whether to back the intention of using AI for the detection.

For classifying the text, Naive Bayes and SVM have been used. The parameters have been fine tuned to improve accuracy.
